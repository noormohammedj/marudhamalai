The Problem
Every pilgrim asks the same
4 questions nobody answers.
500 million pilgrimages happen in India every year. Not one has reliable real-time digital information. The physical temple visit is the most underserved moment in faith-tech.
⏰
"Is the temple open right now?"
No live darshan status anywhere online. Devotees drive 50km only to find a 3-hour queue or a festival closure. The government HRCE portal shows static timings from 2019.
🙏
"Can I book an abhishekam from Dubai?"
NRI families want to arrange poojas at hometown temples for parents. Zero digital booking infrastructure at 99% of temples. WhatsApp to random numbers is the current solution.
🚗
"Where do I park and where do I stay?"
Every pilgrim Googles the same questions and gets 5 stale blog posts. Zero integrated visit planning exists anywhere online today.
🗣️
"I don't read English — will this work for me?"
Every existing temple platform is English-first. Tamil-speaking devotees — the majority at Tamil Nadu temples — are second-class users on their own sacred sites.
The real gap: The government built a digital brochure. Sri Mandir ($50M raised) built a virtual puja app for NRIs. Nobody built the tool for the person actually going to the temple tomorrow morning, in Tamil. That's SacredSpace.

Business Model
Three phases.
Each one funds the next.
We don't charge devotees for what was always free. We monetize the commercial ecosystem that exists because devotees visit — local businesses, travel operators, and marketplace vendors.
🛕
Phase 1 · Month 1–6 · Active Now
Temple Intelligence + WhatsApp Concierge
Local business listings ₹500–2,000/month. NRI pooja facilitation ₹500–1,500/booking. WhatsApp subscriber list building. Live darshan status + visit planning.
Target: ₹48,000/month by M6
🏨
Phase 2 · Month 6–13 · Scale
Travel & Accommodation Commission
Temple circuit packages (CBE 2-day circuit). Hotel/guesthouse bookings (10–15% commission). Transport partnerships. ₹3,000–8,000 revenue per family.
Target: ₹1.5L–2.5L/month by M13
🛒
Phase 3 · Month 13+ · Platform
Temple Ecosystem Marketplace
Prasad ordering, flower/offering pre-orders, vendor storefronts, pandit booking. 15–20% marketplace commission. Fully multilingual — devotee transacts in Tamil.
Target: ₹5L–10L/month by M18
Why this order matters: Phase 1 builds the trust and user base at near-zero cost. Phase 2 monetizes the journey — where family spend is highest. Phase 3 monetizes the ecosystem once traffic justifies vendor investment. This is not a "build and hope" model — each phase pays for the next one.
