🅿️
Parking
New parking plaza at foothills. ₹30–50 for cars. Fill up by 9 AM on weekends.
🚶
Temple Shuttle
Buses every 15 mins from foothills to top. Last bus 7:30 PM. ~₹15 fare.
⛰️ Climbing the Steps
~837 steps from foothills. Best before 8 AM or after 4 PM. Steps close at 5 PM. Carry water — it gets warm. Comfortable footwear strongly advised. Elderly visitors: take the shuttle bus.
